# ADR 0003: Deployment strategy choice

## Status
Not yet decided — to be filled in during Stage 5.

This will compare rolling, blue-green, and canary deployments once the app
exists and there's a real workload to deploy, and will document which
strategy (or combination) was chosen and why, including comparison to the
hand-built canary traffic-splitting logic from the earlier MLOps canary
platform project.
